## Scrap metal & recycling news

> **Page brief.** Target keyword: `scrap metal news`. Audience: yard operators, sellers, traders, journalists, and procurement teams who need a current read on the trade. Funnel stage: awareness. The page should answer: what's moving the scrap and recycling market right now, who the major actors are, and where to find ongoing coverage.

This hub gathers ongoing coverage of the scrap metal and recycling trade — current pricing dynamics, regulatory changes, mill and buyer announcements, tariffs and exports, sustainability reporting, and equipment news. It's the dated companion to the evergreen [Industry Guide](/industry-guide); use the categories below for topic-specific feeds.

## How news on this site is sourced

Articles cite primary sources — SEC filings, mill press releases, federal register notices, AISI/AMM data, and on-the-record industry interviews — rather than aggregating other outlets. When a piece references a price level or a regulatory deadline, the underlying source is linked. When a development is rumor or analyst commentary, it's labeled as such.

## Frequently asked questions

**How often is this section updated?**

Cadence varies by category. Market Commentary moves daily-to-weekly; Mills & Buyers and Regulation are event-driven; Sustainability and Tech & Equipment are slower beats with deeper articles every few weeks.

**Is this a substitute for trade publications?**

No — AMM, Recycling Today, and Scrap Magazine remain the primary trade outlets. This section provides context and analysis aimed at the seller, scrapper, and small-yard reader rather than at corporate procurement.

**Where can I see live prices?**

The [home page](/) carries the live price table for all six metals; each metal has its own live-pricing pillar with 30-day charts.
