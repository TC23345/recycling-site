## Scrap metal & recycling news

Ongoing coverage of the scrap metal and recycling trade — current pricing dynamics, regulatory changes, mill and buyer announcements, tariffs and exports, sustainability reporting, and equipment news. For background and reference material on the same topics, see the [Industry Guide](/industry-guide).

## Sourcing

Articles cite primary sources — SEC filings, mill press releases, federal register notices, AISI/AMM data, and on-the-record industry interviews — rather than aggregating other outlets. Price levels and regulatory deadlines link to the underlying source. Rumor and analyst commentary are labeled as such.

## What you won't find here

## Frequently asked questions

**How often is the news updated?**

Cadence varies by category. Market Commentary moves daily-to-weekly; Mills & Buyers and Regulation are event-driven; Sustainability and Tech & Equipment are slower beats with deeper articles every few weeks.

**Is this a substitute for trade publications?**

No — AMM, Recycling Today, and Scrap Magazine remain the primary trade outlets. The coverage here is aimed at yard operators, sellers, and scrappers rather than at corporate procurement.

**Where can I see live prices?**

The [home page](/) carries the live price table for all six metals; each metal has its own live-pricing pillar with 30-day charts.
