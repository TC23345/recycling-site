## How much is my metal worth?

The scrap and recycling industry moves billions of pounds of metal each year, and the people who navigate it best — sellers, scrappers, yard operators, recycling-center customers — share one thing: they understand what their material is worth before they hit the scale.

This site is built for them. We track current pricing for the most-traded scrap metals, profile major buyers, walk through the practical mechanics of recycling each metal grade, and help readers find reputable yards in their area.

All pricing on this site is indicative — sourced from public futures data with typical scrap discounts applied. Confirm with a local yard before you transact.
