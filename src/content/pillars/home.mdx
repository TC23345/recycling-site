## A practical guide to scrap metal and recycling

The scrap and recycling industry moves billions of pounds of metal each year, and the people who navigate it best — sellers, scrappers, yard operators, recycling-center customers — share one thing: they understand what their material is worth before they hit the scale.

This guide is built for them. We track current pricing for the most-traded scrap metals, profile major buyers, walk through the practical mechanics of recycling each metal grade, and help readers find reputable yards in their area.

## How to use this site

Pick the cluster that matches what you came for:

- **[Copper & Metals Pricing](/copper-price)** — daily price guides for copper, aluminum, brass, and stainless. Start here if you want to time a sale or estimate a load.
- **[Scrap Metal](/scrap-metal)** — the recycling chain itself: how grades work, what removal services charge, and why prices move.
- **[Scrap](/scrap)** — broad scrap fundamentals, including vendor profiles for the largest buyers.
- **[Scrap Yards Near You](/scrap-yard-near-me)** — finding a buyer locally, what to expect on the scale, and how to compare offers across yards.

All pricing on this site is indicative — confirm with a local yard before you transact.
