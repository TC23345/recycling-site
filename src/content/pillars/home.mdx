## How much is my metal worth?

The scrap and recycling trade moves billions of pounds of metal each year. Whether the question is about a garage cleanout, a job-site load, a yard's daily price sheet, or an inherited piece of jewelry, it's the same one: what's it actually worth?

Whats My Scrap Worth? gives you an honest read. Live pricing for the metals most people deal with — copper, aluminum, brass, stainless, gold, silver — paired with plain-English guides on grading, recycling, where the metal goes, and what to expect at a yard. No buyer relationships clouding the take.

All pricing on this site is indicative — sourced from public futures data with typical scrap discounts applied. Confirm with a local yard before you transact.
