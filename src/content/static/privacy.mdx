## Overview

Whats My Scrap Worth? is a general-audience information site providing scrap metal pricing, recycling guides, and educational content. This Privacy Policy explains how the site handles information when you visit.

## Information collected

This site does not collect personal information. There are no accounts, contact forms, newsletter signups, comment sections, or transactions. The site delivers static content over HTTPS.

## Cookies and local storage

Whats My Scrap Worth? does not set tracking cookies, advertising cookies, or third-party tags. The site uses a single browser local-storage key (`theme`) to remember a light/dark mode preference. This data stays on your device and is never transmitted.

## Analytics

This site currently does not run analytics. If a privacy-respecting analytics tool is added in the future, this policy will be updated before it is enabled.

## Third-party services

Live pricing displayed on the site is sourced from public commodity data feeds accessed server-side. Your browser does not connect to those feeds directly, and your IP address is not exposed to them through normal use of this site.

The site is hosted on a third-party hosting provider that may retain standard request logs (IP address, user agent, request URL, timestamp) for security, abuse prevention, and operational purposes per the host's own privacy practices.

## Children's privacy

This site is general-audience content and is not directed at children under 13. No information is knowingly collected from any visitor, including children.

## Your rights

Because no personal information is collected, there is nothing on this site to access, correct, port, or delete. Requests related to third-party hosting logs should be directed to the relevant provider.

If material changes are made to this page, the page and the date stamp below will be updated.
